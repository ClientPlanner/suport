What it is

Client Planner helps professionals who work with clients stay on top of their day—without chaos, missed appointments, or mental overload.

Plan in minutes, find client details instantly, and get timely reminders so your schedule runs smoothly—even on busy days.

Key features

  • Fast appointment creation, edits, and rescheduling
  • Client cards with notes and important details
  • Smart search to find what you need in seconds
  • Optional reminders for tomorrow and unconfirmed appointments
  • Backups and export options so important data stays safe

Who it’s for

Beauty masters, private practitioners, coaches, tutors, freelancers, and anyone who schedules people—not just tasks.
