3) Backups, restore, and exports

iCloud backup

  • Backups are saved to iCloud Drive under your Apple ID.
  • If iCloud Drive is disabled, backup creation may fail.
  • If a new backup fails, the previous backup is kept whenever possible.

Restore

  • Restore is designed as a ‚Äúreplace current data‚Äù action to avoid duplicates.
  • If you need help before restoring, email us ‚Äî include a screenshot of your backup list.

Export

  • Exports create a file and let you choose a destination (Files, email, messenger, etc.).
  • Once exported, the file is stored/handled by the destination you choose.
Tip

If you‚Äôre switching phones, do a backup on the old device first, then restore on the new device.
